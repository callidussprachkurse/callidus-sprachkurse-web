Certification
CILS — your official Italian certificate

CILS stands for Certificazione di Italiano come Lingua Straniera — the official certificate for Italian as a foreign language. It is issued by the University of Siena and recognised by the Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs as well as the Ministry of Education.

The certification system follows the Common European Framework of Reference and comprises six progressive levels. Each level stands on its own and demonstrates your ability to communicate in concrete social, professional and academic contexts. The foundation levels A1 and A2 are divided into flexible units so that you achieve the best possible result by the end of the course.

The exam is developed and assessed by the certification centre of the University of Siena and is held at accredited institutions. It comprises five parts:

Listening comprehension Reading comprehension Language awareness Writing Oral presentation

At CALLIDUS we prepare you specifically for each of these components. Registration for the official exam is handled through the Italian Cultural Institute Hamburg.
